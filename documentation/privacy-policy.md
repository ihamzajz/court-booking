# BookFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

## Overview

BookFlow helps users request court and event bookings. To provide these services, the app stores limited account and booking information.

## Information We Collect

- Account details such as name, username, email address, and optional club/member number.
- Authentication information such as encrypted passwords and session data.
- Booking data such as court bookings, event bookings, status history, and payment status.
- Admin-uploaded media such as court, event, slide, and news images.

## How We Use Information

- To create and manage user accounts.
- To process booking requests and show booking history.
- To let administrators review, approve, reject, and manage bookings.
- To publish app content such as slides, FAQs, news, courts, and events.
- To protect accounts and improve app security.

## Data Sharing

BookFlow does not sell personal information. Data is only shared with authorized administrators and service providers required to operate the platform, such as hosting, database, and email delivery providers.

## Data Retention

We keep account and booking information only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce policies.

## Account Deletion

If you create an account in BookFlow, you can request deletion directly inside the app from the Profile screen by selecting `Delete Account`.

When an account is deleted:

- Access to the account is removed.
- Associated account records are deleted from the application database unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or accounting reasons.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect account information, including encrypted password storage and authenticated access controls.
